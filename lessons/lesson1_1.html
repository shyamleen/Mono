What is Accounting?

Accounting operates uniformly as the "universal language of business." It is a systematically standardized data framework created to "measure, record, analyze, and classify" financial information across platforms. Without a method to normalize transaction values, managers wouldn't know how their company was doing.

Chronological Recording

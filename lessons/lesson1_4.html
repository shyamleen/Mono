Anatomy of a Journal Entry

Every standard journal entry requires five core components to be legally complete. Click the interactive cards below to highlight where they live on a real general journal page!

📅 1. Date The exact day the financial exchange took place.
📖 2. Accounts Debits on top. Credits indented below.
➕ 3. Debit The numeric value in the left column.
➖ 4. Credit The numeric value in the right column.
📝 5. Description A brief note explaining the business purpose.

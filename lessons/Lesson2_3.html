2. How to Make a Balance Sheet (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: Choose a Date and Format

Select the specific "as of" date for your snapshot. A balance sheet is a point-in-time report, unlike the Income Statement which covers a period.

Step 2: List and Categorize Assets

Organize resources by Liquidity. Start with Cash, then Accounts Receivable, then fixed assets like Equipment.

Total Assets = Current + Non-Current

Step 3: Record Liabilities

Categorize by due date. Current (due < 1 year) and Long-Term (due > 1 year).

Step 4: Calculate Shareholders' Equity

Use the proven calculation: Equity = Total Assets - Total Liabilities. This ensures your net worth is accurate.

Step 5: Review and Balance

Check the Golden Rule:

Assets must = Liabilities + Equity

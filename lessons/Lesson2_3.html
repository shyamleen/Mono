The Balance Sheet

The Balance Sheet acts as an instant snapshot of what your business owns, what it owes, and how much is remaining for the investors. This structural layout always satisfies the legendary Accounting Equation: Assets = Liabilities + Equity.

2. How to Make a Balance Sheet (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: Choose Date & Format

Set up an exact date. It must display financial accounts at that single point in time.

Step 2: List and Categorize Assets

List current assets (cash, A/R, inventory) followed by non-current fixed physical items (property, equipment).

Step 3: Organize Liabilities

Group debts by due date: current liabilities and long-term liabilities.

Step 4: Review and Match Equation

Apply the calculation: Equity = Assets - Liabilities. Ensure Total Assets matches Total Liabilities + Total Equity perfectly!

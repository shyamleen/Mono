2. Building the Report Step-by-Step

Step 1: The As-Of Date

Select your snapshot date. This captures balances at exactly that moment.

Step 2: Asset Categorization

List Assets by Liquidity. Cash first, then A/R, then physical property like land.

Step 3: Liability Recording

Group by Maturity. Current (under 1 year) and Long-Term (over 1 year).

Step 4: The Balancing Act

Final check: Total Assets must exactly equal Total Liabilities + Equity.

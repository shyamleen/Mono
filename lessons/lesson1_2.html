Lesson 1.2: The Core Accounting Equation

Every financial transaction impacts a company's fundamental foundational balance scale equation. This relationship ensures that what you own matches exactly how you paid for it.

The Core Formula
Assets = Liabilities + Owner's Equity

* Assets: Resources owned by your business that carry future economic utility value (like Cash or Inventory).
* Liabilities: External obligations and debts owed to lenders, banks, or vendors.
* Equity: The remaining clean insider value left for shareholders after all debts are zeroed out.
